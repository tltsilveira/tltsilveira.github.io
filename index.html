About me

Thiago Lopes Trugillo da Silveira is an Assistant Professor at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS), Brazil. He holds a D.Sc. degree in Computer Science (2019) from UFRGS, an M.Sc. degree in Computer Science (2016), and B.Sc. degrees in Information Systems (2015) and Computer Science (2013) from the Federal University of Santa Maria (UFSM), Brazil. His current interests include pattern recognition, computer vision, and signal and image processing.
