Résumé

Thiago Lopes Trugillo da Silveira is a Professor at Instituto Federal de Educação, Ciência e Tecnologia Sul-rio-grandense (IFSul), Brazil. Thiago holds a PhD degree in Computer Science (2019) from Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS). He also holds an MSc degree in Computer Science (2016) and BSc degrees in Information Systems (2015) and Computer Science (2013) from the Federal University of Santa Maria (UFSM), Brazil. Thiago was the Featured Student in Computer Science (2013/2 - UFSM), recognized by the Brazilian Computer Society (SBC), and received the Odelar Leite Linhares Award of the Best Master Thesis granted by the Brazilian Society of Computational and Applied Mathematics (SBMAC). Thiago has benefited from many research scholarships funded by the brazilian government, namely CNPq (2010 - 2012, 2017 - 2019), RNP (2012 - 2013), FIT-UFSM (2013) and CAPES (2014 - 2017). His current interests include computer vision, pattern recognition and digital signal and image processing.
