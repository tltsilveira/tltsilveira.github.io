About me

Thiago Lopes Trugillo da Silveira is an Assistant Professor ("Professor Adjunto") at the Department of Applied Computing from the Federal University of Santa Maria (UFSM), Brazil. He holds a D.Sc. degree in Computer Science (2019) from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS), Brazil, an M.Sc. degree in Computer Science (2016), and B.Sc. degrees in Information Systems (2015) and Computer Science (2013) from UFSM. Member of the Brazilian Computer Society (SBC), Brazilian Society of Applied and Computational Mathematics (SBMAC), and the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). Head of the Visual Computing Research Group (VisCo) at UFSM. Senior Editor at the Journal of the Brazilian Computer Society (JBCS). His interests include signal, image, and video processing, pattern recognition, and computer vision.
